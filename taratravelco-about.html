About Tará Travel Co.

Tara na! Let's go — together.

We're a Da Nang-based travel concierge building tailor-made trips across Vietnam, one itinerary at a time.

Our Story

Why we started Tará

"Tara na!" is something every Filipino has said a hundred times — it means "let's go," said the moment before an adventure actually happens. That's the feeling we wanted to build a business around.

We're based in Da Nang, Vietnam, and we plan trips for travelers who want more than a hotel booking and a printed itinerary. Most of our guests are Filipino families, or visitors from the US and Australia, who want someone on the ground who actually knows the roads, the guides, and the places worth stopping for.

So we built Tará around one idea: you tell us what kind of trip you want, and we handle everything else — private cars, licensed local guides, hotel picks, and a plan that flexes when your plans do. You show up. We've already taken care of the rest.
