“Tara!” means “Let’s go!” in Filipino — and we added the acute accent to give it a little Vietnamese twist.

Founded in 2023 by Marielle Catral, Tará Travel Co. was born out of a desire to eliminate the stress of travel planning so you can focus on simply living in the moment. Marielle has lived in Vietnam for nearly a decade and knows it by heart. Partnering with a trusted local network, her team creates hassle-free and customized private trips tailored exactly to how you want to travel.

We believe your vacation should actually feel like a vacation.

Our team knows how much time and energy goes into planning a trip. Between researching logistics, booking transportation, and figuring out what to do, the joy of anticipation can easily get lost. Our mission is to shift that focus. We handle all the heavy lifting, complex logistics, and meticulous planning so you don't have to. Your only task is to share your travel interests with our team, and just show up.
