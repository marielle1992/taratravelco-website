Trip Highlights

Why you'll love this trip

Hoi An Ancient Town — a UNESCO World Heritage Site glowing golden at night

My Khe Beach and Son Tra Peninsula — Da Nang's iconic coastline

Coconut Village basket boat and lantern boat ride on the Thu Bon River

Ba Na Hills — Golden Bridge, French Village, and Fantasy Park above the clouds

Day by Day

Your journey

Day 1
Arrival in Da Nang
Airport pickup
Da Nang
▾

Touch down at Da Nang International Airport and your private driver will be waiting. Check in to your hotel, freshen up, and take a slow evening walk along the Han River — the Dragon Bridge lights up at night and is the perfect first taste of the city.

Day 2
Da Nang City
Full day
Breakfast
Da Nang
▾

A full private day in Da Nang with your guide. Visit Linh Ung Pagoda on Son Tra Marina, home to the iconic 67-metre Lady Buddha with panoramic sea views. Climb the Marble Mountains — five limestone peaks with ancient caves and hilltop pagodas. Walk to the Pink Church, browse Han Market, and end the evening watching the Dragon Bridge breathe fire.

Linh Ung Pagoda Son Tra Marina Marble Mountains Pink Church Han Market Dragon Bridge
Day 3
Hoi An Ancient Town
Full day
Breakfast
Da Nang
▾

Drive south to Hoi An and start with a basket boat ride through the Coconut Village — circular woven boats weaving through water-palm canals. Then wander the UNESCO-listed Ancient Town: cross the 400-year-old Japanese Covered Bridge, explore silk and lantern shops, and as the sun drops, board a lantern boat on the Thu Bon River. Hoi An after dark is something else entirely.

Coconut Village Basket Boat Hoi An Ancient Town Japanese Bridge Thu Bon River Lantern Boat
Day 4
Ba Na Hills
Full day
Breakfast, buffet lunch
Da Nang
▾

Ride the cable car up to Ba Na Hills Resort — 1,400m above sea level — and spend the day exploring the clouds. Walk the iconic Golden Bridge, stroll through the Jardin d'amour and French Village, and explore Fantasy Park (indoor amusement park included). Pop Mart collectors, there's a shop here too. A buffet lunch is included.

Ba Na Hills Cable Car Golden Bridge Jardin d'amour French Village Fantasy Park Pop Mart
Day 5
Departure Day
Breakfast
Airport drop-off
▾

A final relaxed morning — sleep in or take a last walk on My Khe Beach. Your private driver will take you to Da Nang International Airport for your flight home.
