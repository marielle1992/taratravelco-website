Weather changes quickly, but we found forecasts to be reliable.

  • The coast is breezier than inland Hoi An and Hue.
  • Dry season (Feb–Aug) = warmest, driest, busiest with travellers.
  • Shoulder months (Feb–Mar) = warm days with noticeably fewer crowds.
  • Typhoon season (Sep–Dec) = heaviest rain, especially around Hue.
  • Ba Na Hills stays noticeably cooler year-round thanks to the altitude.
